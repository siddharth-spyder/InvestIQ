What is Investing?

Investing means using money to buy things that can grow or produce more money in the future.

Real-life analogy:

Like planting a tree now so you get shade and fruit later.

Here's the simple breakdown:
  • Spending = money gone now.
  • Saving = money safe but grows slowly.
  • Investing = money working for you, but with ups and downs.
